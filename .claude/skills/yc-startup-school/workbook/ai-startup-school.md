## Questions

1. Is your product AI-native or AI-wrapped? What would it look like to re-think it from scratch around AI? (Karpathy: Software 3.0)
2. What is your iteration speed right now — how many product changes can you ship per week? (Andrew Ng: iteration speed as the new moat)
3. Are you building on top of foundation models or training your own? What drives that bet? (Jared Kaplan: scaling laws)
4. What is your data flywheel — does using your product make it better for future users? (Aravind Srinivas: AI-native distribution advantage)
5. Where does your product sit on the spectrum from demo to defensible? What makes it hard to replicate? (panel: defensibility)
6. Which of the 6 AI themes from the school is most directly relevant to your product — and which is most threatening to your current approach?
